Data Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account data stored by MV2 (including settings, blocked lists, or browsing history synced via Firebase), please use the form below to generate a deletion request.

Note: Deleting your data in MV2 will not affect your V2EX account. It only removes data specific to the MV2 application.

注意： 删除 MV2 中的数据不会影响您的 V2EX 账号。它仅移除 MV2 应用特有的本地或云端配置数据。
